TOP SHOPPING PLACES IN MUMBAI

PHOENIX PALLADIUM

Phoenix Palladium, India's first premium luxury retail and entertainment at Lower Parel, Mumbai, presents an environment that spells refined elegance, while shoppers soak in the retail offerings, restaurants, cafes, spas and more! Phoenix Palladium houses over 250+ premium brands sprawled across millions of square feet.These include international brands like GUCCI, BURBERRY, COACH,MASSIMO DUTTI, HUGO BOSS, FRANCK MULLER, HUBLOT, PANERAI & many more.
LOCATION: LOWER PAREL

Shop there
ZAVERI BAZAAR

Are you in the market for some serious bling? Head to Zaveri Bazaar, just north of Crawford Market, for an eyeful of gold, silver, precious gems and opulent jewelry that could take your breath away! Shopping in Mumbai for wedding is never complete without stepping into the labyrinthine streets and by-lanes of Bhuleshwar, where Zaveri Bazaar is located. You could also buy high-end home décor, dinnerware, furniture, toys and brassware here.
LOCATION: MANDVI

Shop there
LOKHANDWALA MARKET

This is where it gets really filmy because it would be tough to walk around without toppling over a celebrity! They’re everywhere, and especially in the very chic Lokhandwala Market, a mishmash of branded stores, street vendors, eateries and residential buildings. If you are planning to shop in Mumbai, you must make a stop at this market for fashionable apparels and accessories, shops selling miles of fabric and tailors who can craft your dream outfit. Also, don’t miss out on the first copies of international brands sold here!
LOCATION: ANDHERI WEST

Shop there
A HEAVEN FOR BOOK LOVERS

Visiting book street is a haven for all those who love to read books.This popular place for street shopping in Mumbai sells new as well as secondhand books on the pavement.You can find books ranging from poetry to academic texts and also don’t forget to show your bargaining skills
LOCATION: Next to Dadar railway station. Tulsi Pipe Road, between Dadar and Parel, in central south Mumbai

Shop there
CP TANK

CP Tank also known as Cawasji Patel Tank is quite popular for bangles that are exquisite, fashionable and very affordable in price. The market is the best place for street shopping in Mumbai, especially if you want to buy bangles of various varieties and different from the ones that are generally found in local markets.The market is located in Bhuleshwar and you would always find loads of women just buying bangles right and left.
LOCATION: Bhuleshwar Road,South Mumbai

COLABA CAUSEWAY

Located in South Mumbai, this lively street market is just a short walk from the Gateway of India. Mainly targeted at tourists, Colaba Causeway boasts an extensive collection of stalls selling artificial jewelry, western wear, shawls, key chains, and much more. The market is especially known for its wide array of antique and junk jewelry.
LOCATION: Churchgate

Shop there
JIO WORLD DRIVE

Mumbai's newest and one of the biggest malls - Jio World Drive, is a world of its own and makes for a great place to spend time, on a weekend.
It has some of the most famous brands, as well as some lesser-known international brands for shopping. There are fine-dining options to serving cuisines from all over the world too. However, the party doesn't stop at just that. There are quite a few fun things you can do to make your visit worth it.
LOCATION: BKC

Shop there
CRAWFORD MARKET

Chaotic, colorful and oh-so-traditional… Crawford Market must be on your list of places for shopping in Mumbai. One of the city’s oldest markets, Crawford Market or Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Mandai as it is officially known now, is known for its wholesale shops selling fruits and veggies, home décor accents, bags, makeup, toys and spices, and hold your breath … pets! It is equally famous for its grand structure,a curious mix of Norman and Flemish architecture, adorned with friezes designed by Lockwood Kipling, father of novelist Rudyard Kipling.
LOCATION: FORT
